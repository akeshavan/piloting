Quality Check for the Reliability Project

Automated neuroimaging algorithms aren't always perfect! This website helps us to quickly check the quality of outputs from segmentation and registration. Choose options on the right to filter images. The options are:

Timepoints; 1 - 6
Scanners: GE, Siemens (Si) and Phillips (Ph)
Algorithm: SIENAX (will add SIENA and Freesurfer)
Type: Type of image from each algorithm.

Click the "Generate" button on the menu to display images.
